Our Story

A Century of
Ligurian Elegance

Grand Azura was born from the dream of Countess Valentina Moretti — a woman who believed that true luxury is not the accumulation of things, but the depth of an experience.

In 1923, she commissioned a clifftop residence overlooking the bay of Portofino, furnished with pieces gathered across a lifetime of travel. What began as a private family home became, over generations, the sanctuary it is today — a five-star hotel that still breathes with the intimacy of a private residence.

Today, the fourth generation of the Moretti family oversees Grand Azura alongside fifty dedicated artisans of hospitality. Every stone has been restored, every room reimagined — yet the soul remains: an unwavering commitment to genuine, unhurried care.
