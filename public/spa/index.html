A Sanctuary
Within a Sanctuary

The Azura Spa is an immersive world of thermal water, mineral-rich sea botanicals, and ancient Ligurian healing traditions — re-imagined through the lens of contemporary luxury wellness.

Our 2,400 square metre spa occupies the entire lower level of the hotel, with direct sea views from the thermal suites and outdoor treatment terraces. Water sourced from the Ligurian coast is the heart of every ritual here.
