## Privacy Policy

*Last updated: January 2025*

Grand Azura Hotel & Spa ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with GDPR (EU) 2016/679 and applicable Italian data protection law.

### Data We Collect
We collect information you provide directly (booking details, contact form submissions, newsletter subscriptions) and technical data (cookies, IP address, browser type) when you use our website.

### How We Use Your Data
Your data is used solely to process reservations, respond to enquiries, send requested communications, and improve our services.

### Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, delete or port your personal data. Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@grandazura.com.

For full details, contact us at: **info@grandazura.com**
