## A Century of Ligurian Elegance

Grand Azura was born from the dream of Countess Valentina Moretti — a woman who believed that true luxury is not the accumulation of things, but the depth of an experience.

In 1923, she commissioned the construction of a clifftop residence overlooking the bay of Portofino, furnishing it with pieces gathered across a lifetime of travel.

Today, the fourth generation of the Moretti family oversees Grand Azura alongside a team of fifty dedicated artisans of hospitality. Every stone has been restored, every room reimagined — yet the soul remains unchanged.
