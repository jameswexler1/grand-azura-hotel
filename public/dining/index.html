Il Tramonto Bar

As the sun dissolves into the sea, Il Tramonto comes alive. Our mixologists craft signature cocktails with locally-foraged botanicals, rare Italian vermouths, and artisanal spirits from the Ligurian hills.

Our bar programme celebrates the extraordinary botanicals of Liguria — wild juniper from hillside forests, hand-harvested lemons from the Amalfi terraces, and vermouths aged in century-old barrels.

Al Fresco · Sea Views · 12:30 – 14:30 & 19:00 – 22:30

La Terrazza

Al fresco dining at its most spectacular. La Terrazza serves a curated à la carte menu with uninterrupted sea views. Perfect for a leisurely lunch or a romantic dinner under the stars.

Perched at the very edge of the headland, La Terrazza offers sixty seats under a canopy of olive trees, each table commanding an unbroken view of the Ligurian sea. The menu is lighter, freer, and perfectly suited to long summer lunches and candlelit evenings.
